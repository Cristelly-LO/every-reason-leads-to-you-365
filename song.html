CHAPTER THREE

Our Song 🤍

They say that years from now, we'll forget the little things.

Maybe we'll forget what we wore that day. Maybe we'll forget what we ate. Maybe we'll even forget the exact date.

But I know I'll never forget how you made me feel.

You made an ordinary girl believe that she deserved a love like this.

You became my safest place, my answered prayer, and my greatest blessing.

So whenever this song plays…

I'll always think of you. Because somewhere between every lyric and every heartbeat…

I found my home.

And my home has always been you. 🤍
