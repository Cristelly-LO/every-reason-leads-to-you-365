CHAPTER TWO

Our Favorite Memory 🤍

Every love story has one picture... that quietly becomes home.

Our favorite picture. 🤍

Out of every picture we've ever taken... this one will always be my favorite.

Not because it's perfect. Not because everything around us was perfect. But because every time I look at it... I remember exactly how I felt.

I remember feeling safe. I remember feeling loved. I remember silently thanking God... because the person I had been praying for... was finally standing right beside me.

This picture reminds me that the happiest moments aren't always the loudest ones. Sometimes... they're simply the moments where I get to be with you.

If someone ever asked me what happiness looked like... I'd simply show them this picture. Because somewhere inside this moment... I found my home. And my home has always been you. 🤍
